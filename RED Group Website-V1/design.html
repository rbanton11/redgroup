Design Consulting
Built Around
the Numbers.

Most design decisions in land development aren't aesthetic — they're financial. Unit layout, density configuration, and finish-level targeting directly affect absorption speed, cap rate, and total project return.

The RED Group's design consulting sits at the intersection of engineering, market analysis, and development strategy. We help you make design decisions that are defensible, cost-efficient, and positioned for your target buyer or tenant.

🗺️

Site Layout & Density Optimization

We analyze your parcel for maximum allowable density, efficient circulation, parking ratios, and setback compliance — recommending a layout that maximizes buildable area without regulatory friction.
