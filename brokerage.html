Common Questions

Brokerage FAQ

What is your commission structure?+
Our commission is competitive and transparent, discussed upfront during your consultation. We believe in earning our fee through results — no surprises at the closing table.
Do you offer cash offers for distressed properties?+
Yes. Through our wholesale and investor network, we can often present cash offers on properties in any condition — typically closing in 14–30 days.
How long does it typically take to sell?+
Well-priced Georgia properties in our network typically go under contract within 7–21 days. Timeline depends on condition, pricing, and market conditions — we'll give you a realistic picture upfront.
Do I need to make repairs before listing?+
Not necessarily. We'll advise on which improvements yield the highest ROI and when an as-is listing or cash sale makes more financial sense for your situation.
Can you help with commercial real estate?+
Absolutely. Our brokerage handles office, retail, warehouse, and multi-family commercial transactions across the greater Georgia area.
